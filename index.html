MyoTronic

Revolutionizing Post-Operative Muscle Recovery with At-Home Electrical Stimulation Therapy

Features

Key Features of MyoTronic

Our device combines electrical stimulation with user-friendly design to assist post-operative movement and reduce rehabilitation time.

Non-Invasive Design

No surgery required. Our conductive pads allow for controlled electrical stimulation therapy at home without the high costs of physical therapy.

Targeted Stimulation

Assists quadricep activation and knee stability during heel-to-toe gait for post-operative rehabilitation and assistance.

Comfort-Focused

Designed with failsafes and patient desires in mind for reliable at-home therapy.

Future Expandable

Potential for enhanced motor control and machine-learning based torque pattern recognition in future iterations.

About MyoTronic

Reducing Return-to-Lifestyle Period with Innovation

Post-operative muscle atrophy extends the recovery journey. MyoTronic targets this challenge by providing controlled electrical stimulation therapy that patients can use at home, complementing traditional physical therapy and bridging the gap left by insufficient insurance coverage.

Our device is centered on patient recovery, ensures user safety, and remains cost-effective. It delivers non-invasive muscle activation therapy to restore stability and control, helping patients return to their normal lives faster and more confidently.
